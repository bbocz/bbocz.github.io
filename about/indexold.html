Hello! My name is Benjamin Boczulak. I’m a landscape and architectural photographer based in Cleveland, Ohio. I’ve been drawn to photography as a medium since I was a child as it allows something entirely new to be created from existing scenes. I take photos to challenge my perspective of the world as well as to engage all with the beauty in parts of everyday life. In August, 2019 I published my work in a book entitled Sights, which is sold at Barnes and Noble. This book allows these digital photographs to be brought back to life and blossom into a story of their own.

I am currently a second year student at Miami University studying Marketing. I have always been drawn to this field because of how Marketing provides a relationship and dialogue between the organization and its customers. I believe relationships and people are at the core of business and define successful organizations. Marketing, as a field of study, allows me to analyze these relationships and create mutually beneficial connections that foster growth.

Previously, I have created music which was released globally on iTunes, Apple Music, and Bandcamp. I managed an e-commerce website and fulfilled orders for customers across the US. I built this website from the ground up, which sees over 1,000 unique visitors per month.

My biggest philosophy is that life is not a zero-sum game. Every interaction should be mutually beneficial, as this gives us all the opportunity to grow and learn from each other. If you think we should collaborate please don't hesitate to contact me!
