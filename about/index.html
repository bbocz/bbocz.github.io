My name is Benjamin Ernest Boczulak. I am a marketing major at Miami University in Oxford, Ohio. I grew up and currently reside in Brecksville, Ohio and graduated from Brecksville Broadview Heights High School in 2018. I am best known for my brief dip into the music industry with my cult hit, “Malibu Beaches” (it is available on Apple Music). After “Malibu Beaches” released in 2014, I began many small projects: I started Malibu Money Records and I created a web app that makes it easy to find hiking trails called CAIRN. Architecture, Nature, Photography, and Books all keep me inspired! My biggest philosophy is that life is not a zero-sum game. Every interaction should be mutually beneficial, as this gives us all the opportunity to grow and learn from each other.
