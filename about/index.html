Biography

My name is Benjamin Ernest Boczulak. I am a full time student attending Miami University in Oxford, Ohio. I graduated from Brecksville Broadview Heights High School in June of 2018. At the age of 14 I began experimenting with music production and singing and released my first song “Malibu Beaches” in January of 2014. To my surprise, “Malibu Beaches” generated lots of attention and a cult like following in the Cleveland area. I experimented with music half-heartedly for a few years and released a few more songs, but decided to end my solo career in 2016. However, I continue to produce and master songs for clients. Along the same time frame, I developed a passion for photography and have made many images in film and digital mediums that have seen critical praise. My photographs usually center around the idea of light, color, and patterns in architecture.
