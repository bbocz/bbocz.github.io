Subjuntivo


When to Use Subjunctive?
To give recommendations, suggestions, or demands to another person.
Go to Yo -Drop the O - Add Opposite Vowel - Add Any Addtional Letters Needed

What about Irregulars?

Sepa, sea, vaya, de, este (y), haya!

What about Stem Changers?

Verb's ending in -ar and -er have stem changes in all forms except for the nosotros and vosotros forms!
-ir Verbs also stem change, but only a single stem change occurs in nosotros and vosotros forms!
