View of Bunker Hill Monument

January 15, 2019

by Benjamin Boczulak


Here is a view of Bunker Hill Monument through the trees at Copp's Hill in Boston's North End Neighborhood. This photo was taken in July, 2018. Bunker Hill Monument was created to commemorate the Battle of Bunker Hill which was the first major battle between British and Patriot forces in the American Revolutionary War.
