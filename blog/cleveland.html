CLEVELAND, OHIO

History

Cleveland was founded in 1796 and is centered around where the Cuyahoga River flows into Lake Erie. Historically, Cleveland’s economy was built around manufacturing and transportation. However, today, Cleveland’s economy is centered around manufacturing, healthcare, and financial services. The Greater Cleveland are boasts a population of around 2.1 million.
