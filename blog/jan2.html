Views of Mt. Desert Rock Lighthouse

January 2, 2019

by Benjamin Boczulak


Here is a photo from my archives. It was taken at Mt. Desert Rock Lighthouse in Maine. The photo was taken in 2017. This lighthouse is the furthest offshore and most exposed to the elements compared to all other lighthouses on the east coast. The original structure of the lighthouse was built in 1847.
