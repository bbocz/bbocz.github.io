Sunrise at Cadillac Mountain

January 16, 2019

by Benjamin Boczulak


This is a view of sunrise from the top of Cadillac Mountian. Cadillac Mountain is located in Acadia National Park. Cadillac Mountain is know to be the place in the US to see the sunrise, however, this is only true October through March. This photo was taken in June, 2017.
