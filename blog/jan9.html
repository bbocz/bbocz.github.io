Summer Morning in the Great Smoky Mountians

January 9, 2019

by Benjamin Boczulak


Are you getting the theme yet? Here is yet another photo from my archives. This one was taken in July, 2017 at Great Smoky Mountains National Park near Cades Cove. The Great Smoky Mountains lie between Tennessee and North Carolina and are apart of the Appalachian Mountain Range. The views from Cades Cove are truly amazing, if you are in the area, I recommend you stop by for the day!
