New Book Released!

August 14, 2019

by Benjamin Boczulak


Hi everyone! Today, my book "Sights" is officially released! It is avaliable at Barnes and Noble! The book contains my architecture and landscape photography from the past five years. Locations visited in the book include: Maine, Vermont, New Hampshire, New York, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Ohio, Washington, and Washington DC!
